Web Endeavours

WHAM App
December 2015
“Whats Happening Around Me App” is a new application to provide real time information about events and activities around the city. It has features like details of events in city, advance ticket booking facility, payment options, map view for directions, save user preferences, highlight events based on the user’s likes, ability to share about the event on social media sites and a way to refer your friends for credits.

Stock Watch
June 2016
Stock Watch is a stock portfolio management framework. One can manage multiple portfolios and make analytical decisions for buying or selling stocks based on health of the portfolio as well as performance of the individual stock. It provides analytics of stock health for all users. Experts cast opinions about stock health which users can subscribe to. Each Stock features interactions forum that can help like minded investors to discuss and interact.
